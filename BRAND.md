CLAUDE.md — ChiroTech Evolution
Company Identity
Name: ChiroTech Evolution Tagline: Stop paying for marketing. Start investing in revenue. Mission: To give solo chiropractors the same AI-powered, revenue-focused digital marketing that multi-million dollar practices use — without the bloated contracts, outdated tech, or runaround.

The Problem We Solve
Chiropractors are losing money every month on digital marketing that doesn't perform.
Most solo practitioners are stuck with legacy chiropractic marketing companies that were built in the 2010s and never evolved. These providers sell "packages" stuffed with features that sound impressive — 100+ pages of content, 3D spine simulators, weekly social posts — but none of it is tied to a measurable revenue outcome.
The real pain (use this language in all content):
Contracts that trap, results that don't show up. Chiropractors sign long-term agreements and see little to no increase in new patient bookings. When they ask why, they get vague answers or blame ("SEO takes time," "the algorithm changed").
Overpaying for underperforming marketing. Most are spending $300–$1,500/month and cannot point to a single new patient that came from their website or ads. They don't know their cost-per-lead because nobody is tracking it for them.
Websites that look clinical, not commercial. Legacy providers build template websites that all look the same, use the same stock content, and are not designed to convert visitors into booked appointments. They're brochures, not revenue engines.
Analytics they can't understand. Legacy dashboards show "impressions" and "clicks" — vanity metrics. Chiropractors need to know: How many new patients did my website generate this month? What's my cost per new patient? Which service is driving the most revenue?
Support that deflects instead of delivers. When chiropractors call for help, they get ticket numbers and 48-hour wait times. When they push back on poor results, they're gaslit — told the strategy "needs more time" or that the chiropractor isn't "using the tools correctly."
Old technology dressed up as innovation. Legacy providers bolt on buzzwords like "AI Search Optimizer" to the same aging platform. Under the hood, nothing has changed. The tech stack is from 2015. The websites are built on proprietary CMS platforms that lock chiropractors in and limit what's possible.

Content Creation Rules
When generating ANY content for ChiroTech Evolution — website copy, emails, social posts, proposals, sales decks — follow these rules:
Every piece of content must answer: "How does this make the chiropractor more money?" If it doesn't, rewrite it or cut it.
Lead with the problem, then the solution. Don't start with what we do. Start with what's broken.
Use the chiropractor's language. They say "new patients," not "leads." They say "case value," not "customer lifetime value." They say "adjustments," not "services rendered."
Include a specific, revenue-tied CTA in every piece. Not "Learn more." Instead: "See how many patients your website should be generating → Free Revenue Audit."
Never badmouth competitors by name. Always frame it as "the old way vs. the new way" or "legacy providers vs. modern approach."
Make AI tangible, not buzzwordy. Don't say "we leverage AI." Say "our AI analyzes which of your past patients are most likely to rebook this month, then sends them a personalized message at the right time."
Keep it scannable. Solo chiropractors read marketing materials between patients. Use short paragraphs, bold key numbers, and clear section headers.
Always include the revenue math. Example: "If your website converts just 2% better, and your average new patient is worth $800, that's $X,000/year in additional revenue."
